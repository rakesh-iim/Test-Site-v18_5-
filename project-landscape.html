Masterplanning an Estate

This extensive landscaping project in Whitefield involved a complete redesign of a sprawling estate's grounds. The objective was to create a cohesive landscape design that included formal gardens, recreational areas, and serene water features, all complementing the estate's modern architecture.

We began with extensive earthworks to create dynamic levels, incorporating retaining walls clad in natural stone. The planting design featured a mix of mature specimen trees, sculpted hedges, and vibrant seasonal flower beds, creating year-round visual interest.

The centerpiece of the project is a contemporary koi pond with a cascading waterfall, visible from the main living areas. This comprehensive transformation turned the property into a stunning showcase of landscape architecture and expert execution.
