Balcony Garden Oasis

DLF Camellias, Gurgaon

Transforming Urban Spaces

This premium balcony project at DLF Camellias, Gurgaon, was designed to create a seamless transition between indoor luxury and outdoor serenity. The client wanted a lush, low-maintenance green retreat that offered privacy from neighboring apartments while maximizing the panoramic views of the city.

We incorporated a custom vertical garden to maximize greenery without sacrificing floor space, complemented by automated drip irrigation for effortless upkeep. The decking is crafted from weather-resistant composite wood, paired with bespoke planters that match the apartment's interior aesthetic.

Strategic ambient lighting was integrated into the planters and decking to ensure the space is just as inviting during the evening. The final result is a peaceful oasis in the heart of a bustling urban environment.
