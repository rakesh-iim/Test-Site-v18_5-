Elevated Green Living

This expansive penthouse project required a comprehensive design strategy to turn a barren rooftop into a lush, multi-functional outdoor living space. The client envisioned a resort-style retreat that included areas for dining, lounging, and gardening, all while maintaining spectacular views of the surrounding golf course.

We engineered a structural framework to support heavy planters and a pergola system without compromising the building's integrity. The planting palette focused on hardy, wind-resistant species that thrive in elevated, exposed conditions, creating a vibrant micro-climate.

Key features include a custom-built outdoor kitchen, weather-proof decking, and an integrated smart-irrigation system. The result is a breathtaking sky garden that serves as the perfect escape from city life.
