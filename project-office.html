Toyota Industries, Bangalore

Enhancing Corporate Environments

For Toyota Industries in Bangalore, the goal was to integrate nature into the corporate workspace to boost employee well-being and productivity. We designed an expansive office landscape that seamlessly blended indoor greenery with outdoor relaxation areas.

The project involved creating a central courtyard garden featuring native, low-water plants, walking paths, and shaded seating areas for outdoor meetings. Indoors, we installed a massive living wall in the main lobby, providing a striking visual impact and improving air quality.

Our design prioritized sustainability, utilizing a rainwater harvesting system for irrigation and selecting plant species that require minimal maintenance. The transformed workspace has become a model for biophilic design in corporate environments.
