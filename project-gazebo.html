Villa Project

Gazebo Installation

Prestige Golfshire, Bangalore

Creating an Outdoor Sanctuary

Set against the backdrop of the Nandi Hills, this villa at Prestige Golfshire required an outdoor structure that blended seamlessly with the natural surroundings. We designed and installed a custom wooden gazebo intended for morning yoga, evening lounging, and weekend entertaining.

The structure features premium weather-treated teak wood to withstand Bangalore's varying climate, topped with a slatted roof that allows dappled sunlight to filter through. We surrounded the gazebo with low-maintenance tropical foliage and elegant stepping stones set in manicured turf.

Subtle uplighting was installed around the base of the gazebo and on adjacent trees to create a warm, inviting glow at night. The space has transformed the client's backyard into the primary gathering spot for their family.
